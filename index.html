BCCI

BCCI stands for Board of Control for Cricket in India.

Operating income is $3 billion USD.

The Board of Control for Cricket in India is the governing body for cricket in India and is under the jurisdiction of Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India. The board was formed in December 1928 as a society, registered under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act.


The BCCI organises the following domestic cricket competitions:

  • Ranji Trophy
  • Vijay Hazare Trophy
  • Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy
  • Duleep Trophy
  • Indian Premier League(IPL)
